    • Accounting Course

    Description

    This course focuses on fundamental principles of financial and managerial accounting. Students learn to record financial transactions, prepare financial statements, and analyze financial performance. They also gain insights into budgeting, taxation, auditing, and financial decision-making processes. Accounting skills are essential for individuals pursuing careers in finance, business management, or auditing.
