Arts Department

About the Department

The Arts Department offers a diverse range of courses that promote creativity, critical thinking, and cultural awareness. Students explore various forms of expression, including literature, sociology, history, and more.

Courses Offered

  • English

    The English course focuses on developing language proficiency, literary analysis, and communication skills. Students study various genres of literature, enhance their writing abilities, and engage in critical discussions.

    Learn More

  • Sociology

    Sociology examines human behavior, social structures, and cultural phenomena. Students explore topics such as social inequality, globalization, gender studies, and urbanization. The course encourages critical thinking and societal analysis.
